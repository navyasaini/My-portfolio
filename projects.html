My Projects

Hepatitis C Prediction

The Hepatitis C prediction project entails the development of a machine learning model aimed at forecasting the disease based on a multitude of medical attributes. Our initial steps involved a comprehensive analysis and exploration of the dataset to gain insights into its characteristics and distribution while addressing any missing data. Subsequently, we performed data preprocessing, followed by model training, rigorous evaluation, and fine-tuning. Finally, we leveraged the trained model to make predictions, culminating in a comprehensive assessment of its overall performance.

Multi-Complication Diabetes Detection Model

We developed a singular model capable of detecting diabetes and its associated complications, such as heart disease, vision loss, lower-limb amputation, and kidney disease linked to prolonged high blood sugar levels to facilitate early diagnosis and foster enhanced patient outcomes and medical interventions
